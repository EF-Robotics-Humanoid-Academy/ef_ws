Unitree G1 Academy Zertifikat von EF Robotics

Title: “Unitree G1 Academy – 4-Day Hands-On Intensive Training” 

Location: Hannover, Germany 

Date:16 February 2026 

Duration: 4 days (approx. 09:00–17:00 each day) 

PCapacity: Max. 12 participants (to ensure high hands-on ratio) 

Target audience: Engineers, technicians, project leads from industry, logistics, cleaning robotics, robot integrators – who want to understand, program and deploy the Unitree G1. 

Prerequisites: Basic knowledge of Linux, Python or C++, ideally ROS 2 – because focus is on practical robotics  

 

Objectives: 

Provide solid knowledge of hardware architecture, motion & gait control, navigation, perception and practical application of the Unitree G1. 

Hands-on exercises with the real robot model in small teams. 

Transfer into real-world applications – learn how the Unitree G1 can be used e.g. in industrial/logistics or cleaning robotics settings (and how your company can exploit its potential). 

At the end participants receive a certificate (“Unitree G1 Academy Certificate”) as proof of participation and competence. 
Methodology: Mix of expert lectures (theory), workshops, live demos, team exercises on the real robot model, case-studies from EF Robotics’ practice. 
Agenda (4 days): 

Day 1: Introduction & Fundamentals 

Welcome, EF Robotics overview, Unitree G1 overview 

Hardware architecture & software components of the G1 

Setup, commissioning, safety aspects 

Day 2: Motion Control & Locomotion 

Dynamic walking, stability, control loops 

Reinforcement-learning approaches (analogous to the reference) 

Workshop: Basic gait/control task 

Day 3: Navigation & Environment Awareness 

Mapping, localization, global path planning & local obstacle avoidance 

Sensor integration (e.g., RealSense or equivalent) 

Workshop: Navigation task with obstacle field 

Day 4: Perception, Application & Project Transfer 

Visual perception, object/environment recognition 

Project work: Small challenge – participants implement a real-world application with G1 (e.g., motion + perception task) 

Final team presentations, discussion “How do I deploy it in my company?”, certificate issuance 
Included in price: Training fee, training materials, lunch and coffee/beverages during sessions, certificate, practical access to the robot model. 
Not included: Travel, accommodation, evening events (optional). 
Cancellation policy: (for instance) Full refund up to 30 days before training starts, thereafter 50 % fee, later no refund; EF Robotics retains the right to cancel the training if minimum participant number isn’t reached (with full refund). 
Marketing strategy: Early Bird announcement via newsletter, LinkedIn, paid ads; collaboration with existing customers; testimonials; landing page with booking form; social media posts (stories, event video). 
Venue & Infrastructure: Training room at EF Robotics’ Hannover location with access to robotics lab; good public transport connection; parking available. 
Participant materials: Digital manual, checklists, code samples, access to demo scenarios. 
Follow-up: Six weeks online support via e-mail/Teams session, access to alumni/community group of EF Robotics.
