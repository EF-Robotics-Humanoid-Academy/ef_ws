Safety FAQ (navigation/obstacle_avoidance/safety)
=================================================

Q: What scripts are in this folder?
A: `hanger_boot_sequence.py` and `keyboard_controller.py`.

Q: Why use these before navigation scripts?
A: They provide controlled startup and manual tele-op for positioning before
   running map/planning scripts in this directory.

Q: How do I run keyboard tele-op?
A: `python3 keyboard_controller.py --iface enp1s0`

Q: Input backend options?
A: `--input pynput` (default) or `--input curses`.

Q: Keyboard controls?
A: `W/S` forward/back, `Q/E` strafe, `A/D` yaw, `Space` stop,
   `Z` Damp + exit, `Esc` StopMove + ZeroTorque + exit.

Q: Does this folder include a USB gamepad controller?
A: No. There is no `usb_controller.py` in this folder.

Q: Safety warning?
A: Keep a spotter and clear route before handing control to autonomous
   navigation scripts.
